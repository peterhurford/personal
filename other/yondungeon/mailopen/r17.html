Thou walks north, and finds ye general store. There is a sign that says: "Pirate's General Store." Thou tryisth thy door but finds ye general store closed. The sign on the door says: "Arrr... ye pirate is out to lunch! Come back later..." Thou cannot tell if the sign is written in ye olde english or ye pirate speech, but they art similar enough.

Thou walks north, and finds ye general store. There is a sign that says: "Pirate's General Store." Thou tryisth thy door and it opens great! The sign on the door says: "Ye Pirate is In! WELCOME!" Thou cannot tell if the sign is written in ye olde english or ye pirate speech, but they art similar enough. Furthermore, thou notices a hole that thou has never noticed before.
